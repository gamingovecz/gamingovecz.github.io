Before starting, keep all items in your inventory from both your Bedrock account and your Java account in the crate! Including EnderChest!

How to link a Java account to a Bedrock account?

  1. Read the instructions before you start.
  2. Scroll down
  3. Click the "Login to Bedrock" button
  4. Log in to the account he uses and agree.
  5. You continue to repeat at the button "Login to Java", if it says "logout" everything is already done.
  6. In case it doesn't work here. Click on this link: https://link.geysermc.org/method/online

Not working through the page?

Try it in the game.

  1. Write in the chat: /linkaccount
  2. It will accompany you further
This page has nothing to do with https://link.geysermc.org/method/online. We do not have access to your personal data!
